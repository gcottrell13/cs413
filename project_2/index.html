This Game Really Grinds My Gears

Instructions

How to play:

Get the orange gears to spin up to 100%!
They each have different requirements for how far they have to spin.

Use the grey and yellow gears to do that,
but you can only modify their arrangement when the mechanism isn't running.

Put gears on top of each other to make them rotate with the same speed.

There are bugs when gears of multiple speeds are trying to move each other. They should be locking up, but it doesn't happen.
